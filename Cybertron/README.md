---

## What Is Cybertron Construction?

Cybertron Construction investigates:
- Global energy generation and distribution
- Autonomous infrastructure maintenance
- Planet-wide coordination between machines and humans
- Scalable systems that can evolve with civilization growth

Think of it as treating Earth itself as an engineered system.

---

## Why This Matters for Humanity

Modern civilization already depends on global systems, but they are:
- Fragmented
- Inefficient
- Fragile

A Type 1 Civilization requires infrastructure that is:
- Resilient
- Self-regulating
- Energy-aware at planetary scale

Without this, advanced technology collapses under its own complexity.
